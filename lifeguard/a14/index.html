Migrating to Android 14

LifeGuard

PAGE UPDATED: August 30, 2024

Overview

Migrating Zebra devices to Android 14 from earlier Android versions necessitates multiple upgrade steps. While this process may require careful attention, we are committed to providing guidance to ensure a smooth and efficient transition. The affected devices include SD660-platform and QC6490-platform devices.

Coming soon: Lifeguard OTA will soon offer a seamless solution, delivering these updates in the appropriate order without manual intervention.


QC6490 Devices Upgrade Path

From Android 11

For QC6490 devices on Android 11:

  • If the device is at the September 2024 Android 11 LG version or later, it can be upgraded directly to any Android 14 LG version.
  • If the device is at an LG version older than September 2024 Android 11, follow these steps to upgrade to Android 14:
    1. Upgrade to the minimum Android 11 LG version 11-21-27.00-RG-U00-STD (May 2023).
    2. Upgrade to the initial Android 14 LG version 14-18-19.00-UG-U00.
    3. Upgrade to any Android 14 LG version.

From Android 13

For QC6490 devices on Android 13:

  • If the device is at the September 2024 Android 11 LG version or later, it can be upgraded directly to any Android 14 LG version.
  • If the device is at an LG version older than September 2024 Android 11, follow these steps to upgrade to Android 14:
    1. Upgrade to the initial Android 14 LG version 14-18-19.00-UG-U00.
    2. Upgrade to any other Android 14 LG version.


SD660 Devices Upgrade Path

From Android 11

Devices upgrading from Android 11 to Android 14 must follow the Migrating to Android 13 guide to ensure data integrity during the migration. Once on Android 13, the device can be upgraded to any Android 14 version.

From Android 13

Devices running Android 13 can directly upgrade to any Android 14 version.
