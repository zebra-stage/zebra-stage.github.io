Migrating to Android 14

LifeGuard

Overview

This guide provides important information on upgrading Zebra devices to Android 14, categorized by device platform. While this process may require careful attention, we are committed to providing guidance to ensure a smooth and efficient transition.

For a list of device models based on Zebra platforms, refer to Zebra Platform Devices.


QC6490 Devices Upgrade Path

Migrating Zebra QC6490-platform devices to Android 14 from earlier Android versions necessitates multiple upgrade steps.

From Android 11

For QC6490 devices on Android 11, all devices must be updated to the November 2024 LifeGuard Update or a later version before upgrading to Android 14. Follow these steps:

  1. Upgrade to the November 2024 LifeGuard Update or later version.
  2. Upgrade to any Android 14 LifeGuard Update version.

NOTE: After upgrading to LifeGuard Update released in November 2024 or later, downgrading to an older version will result in loss of user data and installed applications. Refer to the QC6490 OS Update instructions for more information.

From Android 13

For QC6490 devices on Android 13, all devices must be updated to the November 2024 LifeGuard Update or a later version before upgrading to Android 14. Follow these steps:

  1. Upgrade to the November 2024 LifeGuard Update or later version.
  2. Upgrade to any Android 14 LifeGuard Update version.

NOTE: After upgrading to LifeGuard Update released in November 2024 or later, downgrading to an older version will result in loss of user data and installed applications. Refer to the QC6490 OS Update instructions for more information.
