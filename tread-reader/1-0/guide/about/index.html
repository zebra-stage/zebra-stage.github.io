About Tread Depth Reader

Tread Depth Reader 1.0

Overview

Tread Depth Reader is a tire inspection solution that helps improve worker productivity and increases the efficiency of capturing tire tread depth and Department of Transportation (DOT) information. It is a laser-based tire tread depth reader accessory for Zebra's TC75x mobile computer. With the tread depth reader accessory attached to a mobile computer, swipe the device across the surface of the tire to detect and record data. The tire is mounted on a wheel.

This document provides a comprehensive overview of how applications should access Tread Depth Reader functionality. It describes the method(s) in which components should interact and fulfill functional and non-functional requirements for any application focusing on retrieving tread data from the reader accessory.

Requirements

  • Android 11 or higher
  • See Zebra Support Portal for the supported devices list (e.g., TC7x and TC5x series devices)
  • Accessory Tire Tread Depth reader
