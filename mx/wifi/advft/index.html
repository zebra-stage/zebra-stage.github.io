Note: This advanced option is only to be used under the guidance of Zebra Support personal. Do not attempt to use these features on your own, doing so may result in undesirable results.

This is to enable / disable Fast Transition roam algorithm (802.11r). Aggregated FT improves on IEEE 802.11r, Over-the-DS fast roaming. In conjunction with Zebra wireless LAN infrastructure, the device will achieve more reliable and consistent fast roaming.

Default: enabled.
