Used to designate channels in the 6.0GHz band for use by the device. While channels used in this band are generally determined automatically through negotiation with the Wi-Fi infrastructure, channels or sets of channels also can be specified manually to avoid interference with other devices or to dedicate certain channels for specific purposes. Warning: Not all channels are available in all countries. For more information, see the List of WLAN Channels.

See all Zebra devices by platform

Parm value input rules:

  • String from 0–64 characters containing a set of valid channels
  • Multiple values must be separated by commas (i.e. "36,40,48...")
  • Also accepts ranges when specified with a hyphen (-) (i.e. "36-60")
  • Only valid channels are applied, and are subject to local regulatory and/or platform restrictions
