Used to control whether the device responds when the designated Wake Word (default = "okay zebra") or Duress Word (default = "okay pineapple") is spoken by the device user.

The Zebra WS301 device includes the Zebra WakeWord service and SDK, enabling third-party apps to receive wake-word detection callbacks and voice command data, and to process captured audio files. For information about WakeWord integration and app usage, refer to the WS301 Programmer's Guide.
