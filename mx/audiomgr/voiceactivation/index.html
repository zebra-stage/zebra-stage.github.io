Used to control whether the device responds when the designated Wake Word or Duress Word is spoken by the device user.

The Zebra WS301 device includes the Zebra WakeWord service and SDK, enabling third-party apps to receive wake-word detection callbacks and voice command data. For more information about Zebra WakeWord integration, refer to the Zebra WakeWord Integration Guide below.
