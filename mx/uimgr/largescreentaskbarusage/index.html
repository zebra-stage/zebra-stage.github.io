Controls whether the Large-screen Taskbar is visible on supported Zebra devices, including those listed below. When enabled on devices that support it, this enhanced taskbar appears at the bottom of the screen and adds links to the App Drawer and pinned apps.

Supported Devices*

  • CC600 (10.1 inch)
  • ET40, ET45 (10.1-inch only)
  • ET5x (8 inch, 10.1 inch)
  • ET60, ET65 (10.1-inch)
  • L10 (10.1 inch)

* Might be supported by other devices not listed.

Platform Note

This feature requires the following platform-specific OSX versions:
