Used to set a battery-charge percentage below which the charge level is considered critically low. This allows control over how long a device runs before being forced to shut down due to a low battery condition.

A value of "-1" is supported only on devices with MX 13.4 or later; such devices can accept "0" as a critically low threshold value. On MX 13.3 (and earlier), "1" (one percent) is the smallest effective value supported.
