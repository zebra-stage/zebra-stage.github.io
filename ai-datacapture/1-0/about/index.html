About AI Data Capture SDK

AI Data Capture SDK 1.0

Overview

The Zebra AI Data Capture SDK enables developers to create and deploy computer vision applications on Zebra mobile computing devices. Designed for both seasoned AI professionals and beginners, the SDK offers the tools and resources to bring innovative ideas to life. With Java Native Interface (JNI) bindings, it integrates seamlessly with popular programming languages and environments.

SDK capabilities:

  • Localizer - Detects objects in images, including:
    • Barcodes - Detects various barcode types.
    • Retail Shelf Objects - Detects products, shelf labels, peg labels and shelves in full or partial shelf images.
  • Product Recognition - Utilizes Feature Extractor, Feature Storage and Recognizer components to build and store detailed visual data about retail products, enhancing inventory management. This process typically follows the use of Retail Shelf Object Localizer, which detects products in images, crops them and submits them for recognition.
  • Barcode Decoder - Decodes barcodes within images, typically after the Barcode Localizer detects and crops them for recognition. Supports:
    • Single Barcode - Decodes one barcode.
    • Multi-Barcode - Decodes multiple barcodes, detected by the Barcode Localizer.
  • Text OCR - Detects and recognizes text characters in images, capturing them as words or paragraphs.

For more information on each capability, refer to the subsections below.

Requirements

  • Zebra QC6490 mobile computer device with Android 14 or higher

Localizer

Localizer detects objects in images.

  • Barcode Localizer - An AI model that detects 1D and 2D barcodes in images, supporting various use cases from barcodes on boxes of goods to shelves and shipping labels.

    Sample of detected barcodes

  • Retail Shelf Object Localizer Detects and identifies objects on retail shelves, aiding inventory management, optimizing space and ensuring accurate labeling. The types of objects detected include:

    • Products - Identifies individual products on the shelf, facilitating inventory tracking and automating stock checks.
    • Shelf Labels - Detects and reads shelf labels, ensuring that products are accurately priced and labeled.
    • Peg Labels - Recognizes peg labels used for hanging products, aiding in efficient product organization.
    • Shelves - Detects the presence and structure of shelves themselves, helping in understanding shelf layouts and optimizing space usage.

    Sample of objects detected on a retail shelf

Product Recognition

Product Recognition helps build a database for inventory tracking and automated checkouts. The Feature Extractor isolates key features from images to generate descriptors - numerical representations that encapsulate an item's essential characteristics - and stores them in Feature Storage. Once a database of recognizable products is established, the Recognizer performs semantic searches to find similar descriptors, predicting product identities.

Barcode Decoder

The Barcode Decoder decodes barcodes identified by the Barcode Localizer. It utilizes an open-source decoding capability within the API.

Text OCR

The Text OCR model detects and decodes text characters in images, offering suggestions for recognized characters or words. It adapts to various fonts and input sizes, allowing for effective text recognition at different distances. Detected words can be grouped into 'lines' or 'paragraphs.'
