About Zebra DNA Core

Zebra DNA Core

Overview

Zebra DNA Core is a set of tools and features for enhancing productivity, performance and security of Zebra devices running Android. The DNA Core and DNA Core+ tiered software system gives partners flexibility to select full-featured Core+ devices out of the gate, or to acquire Core-series devices to keep initial costs low, and upgrade to Core+ as the organization grows.

  • Zebra Essential-tier devices include DNA Core, the must-have elements of Zebra DNA for app development and device management. Formerly known as Professional-series devices.
  • Zebra Select- and Premier-tier devices include the Zebra DNA Core+, the full suite of enhancements for increased levels of productivity, performance and security. Formerly known as Enterprise-series devices.

Zebra's Essential-series devices (above, left) can be purchased for less than Select and Premier devices and later upgraded with Core+ components as the business grows or needs arise.

Zebra DNA Core Components

Zebra Essential devices in the table above include DNA Core, which delivers the must-have software components listed below.

Zebra Essential devices include:

  • GMS Restricted Mode
  • Mobility Extensions (MX)
  • OEMConfig Powered by MX
  • RxLogger
  • StageNow

* Free download and/or installation required.
