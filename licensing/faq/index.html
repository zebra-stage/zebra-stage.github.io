Core Licensing Basics

What is changing about Zebra's licensing policy?

A: The major changes:

  • Perpetual MDNA licenses are scheduled to be discontinued on Sept. 30, 2026.
  • Effective immediately, Zebra offers only 3- and 5-year term licenses for Core+ Upgrades, replacing the "MDNA Upgrade" and "MDNA Enterprise Upgrade" bundles.
  • Zebra introduces new software "bundles" and new product names for its device tiers:
    • "Essential devices" (formerly "Professional Series") include "DNA Core" bundle.
    • "Select" and "Premier" devices (formerly "Enterprise series") include "DNA Core+" bundle.

What's the difference between DNA Core and DNA Core+?

A: The DNA Core apps (included on Essential-tier devices):

  • DataWedge
  • Device Diagnostics Tool
  • DNA Cloud client*
  • EMDK for Android
  • EMDK for Xamarin/MAUI
  • Enterprise Home Screen*
  • GMS Restricted Mode
  • Mobility Extensions (MX)
  • OEMConfig Powered by MX
  • RxLogger
  • StageNow

The DNA Core+ apps (included on "Select-" and "Premier-tier" devices):

  • All DNA Core apps (above)
  • Bluetooth Manager
  • Certified Mobile Parcel
  • Device Central
  • Enterprise Keyboard*
  • Next-gen SimulScan enhanced features:
    • Free-form OCR
    • Multi-barcode support
  • Terminal Emulation Lite*
  • Wireless Analyzer
  • Wireless Fusion
  • Workstation Connect*
  • Zebra Volume Control

* Free download and/or installation required.

What are the advantages of Term Licensing?

A: Term licensing turns software cost into a predictable, manageable operating expense instead of a large, upfront capital expense. Most importantly, you can match your software license term directly to your hardware's life cycle, so you never pay for software on a device that has been retired.

Do I need to buy a DNA Core+ Upgrade if I purchase a Premier-tier device?

A: No. Premier-tier devices come with the full suite of premium software features built in. The DNA Core+ Upgrade is required only for Essential-tier devices.

What is DNA Advanced?

A: The "DNA Advanced" bundle‡ includes the following:

  • All DNA Core and Core+ apps
  • Device Guardian
  • Enterprise Browser
  • Identity Guardian
  • Wireless Insights
  • Zebra DNA Cloud

‡ Products also may be purchased separately, if desired.

Is DNA Advanced licensed per-device or per-user?

A: DNA Advanced is licensed per device. Each license is tied to a single piece of hardware, making it simple to manage the fleet without tracking individual users.

Are there different versions or SKUs of DNA Advanced depending on the device?

A: No. Zebra offers a single SKU for the DNA Advanced Kit.

Does purchasing the DNA Advanced bundle include data storage for DNA Cloud?

A: No. The DNA Advanced license provides full access to the DNA Cloud platform only; data storage tiers are licensed and sold separately.

Transition and Fleet Management

Can I mix perpetual and term licenses in the same device fleet?

A: Yes. Existing devices with legacy perpetual licenses continue to work as always. Devices with term-based licenses can be added seamlessly to the same fleet.

If a broken device needs replacement, what happens to its term license?

A: The license is retained in the Zebra Licensing Portal, and is easily released from the broken device and reassigned to its replacement for the remainder of the active term.

Renewals, Expiration

What happens when my 3-year or 5-year DNA Core+ term license expires?

A: At license expiration, premium software features unlocked by the license are deactivated and effected device(s) revert to the base DNA Core functionality. To restore access, simply purchase renewal SKU(s) for the effected device(s).

How do I keep track of term-license expiration dates?

A: The easiest way to track license inventory is through the Zebra DNA Cloud dashboard. After loading a license Batch ID, DNA Cloud provides a single view of every license, the device it's assigned to, and its expiration date.

Deployment, Add-ons

Due to constraints of our EMM system, we use custom hardware SKUs with MDNA preinstalled. How can we transition to the term-based model on new device purchases?

A: Next-generation devices natively support downloading DNA Core+ or DNA Advanced term licenses over the air via DNA Cloud or an EMM system during initial-boot staging. If completely offline, a Zebra Support request can be submitted to build a custom SKU with a pre-activated term license.

We have an existing DNA Cloud subscription but want to add Wireless Insights. Do we need to buy the full DNA Advanced bundle?

A: No. For existing devices, a standalone Wireless Insights term license can be purchased and co-termed to match your remaining DNA Cloud duration. However, for new hardware expansions, we recommend purchasing the all-inclusive DNA Advanced Kit to consolidate everything into a single subscription.

Q: Can licenses erased from a device be reused?

A: Yes. Licenses erased by a Factory Reset or other device service process can be reissued to the same device(s) using the same StageNow Profile and the same License Source (i.e. cloud-based or local server).

Q: I already have some licenses in my organization. What's the best way add more?

A: When ordering additional unit entitlements for an existing site license, include the Activation ID with the order to ensure that the same EMM policy can be used for the additional devices.

Q: Does Zebra offer evaluation licenses?

A: Yes. See the Process page to learn how they're acquired and applied.

Q: Are evaluation licenses installed in the same way as regular commercial licenses?

A: Yes. The fastest way to deploy an evaluation license on a device is manually, by using the License Manager app and the manual license assignment procedures.

  • Device licenses can be activated using:
    • StageNow and the License Mgr CSP
    • OEMConfig tool, part of Zebra's EMM Toolkit (available to authorized partners only)

Q: I'm having trouble viewing the MX Feature table on a mobile device. Can that be improved?

A: Yes. Try enabling the "Desktop site" (or similar) option in the mobile browser.
