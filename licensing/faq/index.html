Frequently Asked Questions

Zebra DNA Core

PAGE UPDATED: July 15, 2026

Core Licensing Basics

Is DNA Advanced licensed per-device or per user?

A: DNA Advanced is licensed per device. Each license is tied to a single piece of hardware, making it simple to manage your fleet without tracking individual users.

What are the advantages of Term Licensing?

A: It turns your software cost into a predictable, manageable operating expense (OpEx) instead of a large, upfront capital expense (CapEx). Most importantly, you can match your software license term directly to your hardware's lifecycle, so you never pay for software on a device you've already retired.

Do I need to buy a DNA Core+ Upgrade if I purchase a Premier-tier device?

A: No. Premier-tier devices come with the full suite of premium software features built-in. You only need the DNA Core+ Upgrade for our Essential-tier devices.

Will there be different versions or SKUs of DNA Advanced depending on the device I have?

A: No. To make things simple, there is only one SKU for the DNA Advanced Kit. It is a universal license.

Does purchasing the DNA Advanced bundle include my data storage tiers for DNA Cloud?

A: No. The DNA Advanced license gives you full access to the ZDNA Cloud platform, but data storage tiers are licensed and sold separately.

Transition, Fleet Management

Can I mix perpetual and term licenses in the same device fleet?

A: Yes, absolutely. Your existing devices with legacy perpetual licenses will continue to work exactly as they do now. You can seamlessly add new devices with term-based licenses to the same fleet.

If a device breaks and needs to be replaced (RMA), what happens to my term license?

A: Your license is not lost. Through the Zebra Licensing Portal, you can easily release the license from the broken device and reassign it to the new replacement device for the remainder of its active term.

Renewals, Expiration

What happens when my 3-year or 5-year DNA Core+ term license expires?

A: The premium software features unlocked by the license will be deactivated on that specific device, which will revert to its base DNA Core functionality. To maintain access, you simply purchase a new renewal SKU.

How do I keep track of when my term licenses are going to expire?

A: The easiest way is through the ZDNA Cloud dashboard. When you load your license Batch ID, you get a single-pane-of-glass view of every license, the device it's assigned to, and its exact expiration date.

Deployment, Add-ons

We use custom hardware SKUs with MDNA pre-installed due to Intune/EMM constraints. How do we transition to the term-model on next-gen devices?

A: Next-gen devices natively support pulling down DNA Core+ or DNA Advanced term licenses over-the-air via ZDNA Cloud or EMM during initial boot staging. If completely offline, a CASP request can be submitted to build a custom SKU with a term license pre-activated.

We have an existing ZDNA Cloud subscription but want to add Wireless Insights. Do we need to buy the full DNA Advanced bundle?

A: No. For existing devices, you can purchase a standalone Wireless Insights term license co-termed to match your remaining ZDNA Cloud duration. However, for new hardware expansions, we recommend purchasing the all-inclusive DNA Advanced Kit to consolidate everything into a single subscription.

Q: Can licenses erased from a device be reused?

A: Yes. Licenses erased by a Factory Reset or other device service process can be reissued to the same device(s) using the same StageNow Profile and the same License Source (i.e. cloud-based or local server).

Q: I already have some licenses in my organization. What's the best way add more?

A: When ordering additional unit entitlements for an existing site license, include the Activation ID with the order to ensure that the same EMM policy can be used for the additional devices.

Q: Does Zebra offer evaluation licenses?

A: Yes. See the Process page to learn how they're acquired and applied.

Q: Are evaluation licenses installed in the same way as regular commercial licenses?

A: Yes. The fastest way to deploy an evaluation license on a device is manually, by using the License Manager app and the manual license assignment procedures.

  • Device licenses can be activated using:
    • StageNow and the License Mgr CSP
    • OEMConfig tool, part of Zebra's EMM Toolkit (available to authorized partners only)

Q: I'm having trouble viewing the MX Feature table on a mobile device. Can that be improved?

A: Yes. Try enabling the "Desktop site" (or similar) option in the mobile browser.
